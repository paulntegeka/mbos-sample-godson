Data dictionary of the columns in the spreadsheet template to be upload
NIN
Required

This is the National ID Number for the borrower.

Example: CM1234578VUE
Phone Number
Required

This is the phone number of Borrower.

Example: 0789076068
Borrower ID
Required

This is the Borrower Id of the borrower as captured your own internal systems.
Line of business
Required

This is a character descibing the borrowers line of business.

Example: Agriculture, Manufacturing
Gender
Required

This is the gender of the borrower.

Example: Male for Male borrowers, Female for Female borrowers
Age
Required

This is the age of the borrower at the time of loan issue.
Year of loan Issue
Required

This is the year when the loan was issued to the borrower

Example: 2022
Month of loan Issue
Required

This is the Month when the loan was issued to the borrower

Example: 01 for the month of January
Day of loan Issue
Required

This is the day when the loan was issued to the borrower

Example: 01 for the month of January
loan amount
Required

This is the amount lent to the borrower in Uganda shillings
Tenure of loan
Required

This is the expected payback period of the loan in months

Example: 6 to mean 6 months
Interest rate of loan
Required

This is the precentage interest rate of the loan

Example: 11.5 to mean 11.5 percent per annum
Annual revenue of borrower
Required

This is the total revenue that a borrower's business makes per accounting period
Location of borrower
Required

District in which the business for which the borrower is taking money is located

Example: 11.5 to mean 11.5 percent per annum
Expected monthly installment
Required

This is the equal monthly installment that the borrower is expected to make every month for the duration of the loan term
Number of employees
Required

Number of individulas that the borrower's business employs
